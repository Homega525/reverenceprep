Our Curriculum

Subjects Taught Across All Divisions

Our curriculum follows Ghana's official NaCCA/GES Common Core Programme, covering all subjects from Pre-School through Basic 9 — nurturing well-rounded, confident, and capable learners at every stage.

Our Pre-School follows Ghana's four core thematic learning areas — designed to build a strong early foundation through play, creativity, and exploration.

📖

Literacy

Emergent reading, phonics, storytelling, speaking, and listening — building confident communicators from the very start.

🔢

Numeracy

Counting, shapes, patterns, and basic number sense — making mathematics fun and intuitive for young learners.

🎨

Creative Arts

Drawing, music, movement, drama, and imaginative play — nurturing self-expression and creativity in every child.

Primary school introduces a rich and balanced curriculum with core academic subjects, Ghanaian identity, and practical life skills — with French introduced as a standalone subject from Basic 4.

🔤

English Language

Reading, writing, grammar, comprehension, and oral communication — the foundation of academic success.

➕

Mathematics

Numbers, operations, geometry, measurement, and problem-solving — building strong mathematical thinkers.

🔬

Science

Integrated science covering life, physical, and earth sciences — encouraging curiosity and inquiry-based learning.

🗣️

Ghanaian Language

Mother-tongue instruction in Twi, Ga, Ewe, Dagbani and other Ghanaian languages — celebrating our linguistic heritage.

🎭

Creative Arts & Design

Visual art, music, dance, and drama — building imagination, cultural appreciation, and artistic confidence.

🙏

Religious & Moral Education

Moral values, ethics, and religious literacy — nurturing character, integrity, and respectful citizenship.

🏛️

History of Ghana

Ghana's rich heritage, traditions, independence, and national identity — taught as a standalone subject.

💻

Computing / ICT

Digital literacy, basic programming concepts, and responsible technology use — preparing students for a digital world.

🇫🇷

French From Basic 4

Introduced from Basic 4 as a standalone subject — building early foreign language skills and broadening global awareness.

Junior High follows Ghana's Common Core Programme (CCP) — a rigorous 10-subject curriculum preparing students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).

🎓

Students sit the BECE at the end of Basic 9 (JHS 3). Our teachers are dedicated to ensuring every student is fully prepared.

➕

Mathematics

Advanced arithmetic, algebra, geometry, statistics, and logical reasoning — building the analytical minds of tomorrow.

🔤

English Language

Literature, composition, comprehension, grammar, and oral skills — developing articulate and effective communicators.

🔬

Integrated Science

Biology, chemistry, physics, and earth science combined — cultivating scientific thinking and experimentation.

🌐

Social Studies

Civics, geography, history, economics, and Ghanaian identity — building informed and responsible citizens.

💻

Computing / ICT

Programming, digital tools, internet safety, and data literacy — equipping students for the digital age.

🎭

Creative Arts & Design

Visual arts, performing arts, and design thinking — fostering innovation and cultural pride.

🔧

Career Technology

Technical and vocational skills including woodwork, home management, and agricultural science — practical preparation for life beyond school.

🙏

Religious & Moral Education

Ethics, values, and spiritual well-being — shaping young people of strong character and moral conviction.

🗣️

Ghanaian Language

Continued mother-tongue instruction — preserving and celebrating Ghana's linguistic and cultural diversity.

🇫🇷

French

Building on primary French — developing conversational, reading, and writing skills in West Africa's most widely spoken language.
