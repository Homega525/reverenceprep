Admissions Process

  1. Complete the admissions enquiry form with parent and student details.
  2. In some cases, your ward writes an exam to determine the class they will be a good fit for
  3. Submit required documents and previous school records.
  4. Receive placement confirmation and complete enrollment.
  5. Meet the class master of your ward's new class.
