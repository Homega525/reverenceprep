Reverence Preparatory and Junior High School’s Junior High department brought its Cultural and Old School Day celebration to a successful close on vacation day, Friday, 3rd April 2026.

![Students in old-school outfits](/assets/img/uploads/photo_2026-04-03_20-14-58.jpg "Old-school")

The event was filled with excitement, colour and creativity as students proudly appeared in cultural attire and old school-inspired outfits, making the day both memorable and meaningful. It was a wonderful opportunity for learners to celebrate heritage, appreciate tradition and enjoy a fun final activity together before vacation.

The celebration reflected the confidence, unity and creativity of our students, and it was a joyful way to end the term on a high note.

To see more moments from the event, readers are invited to visit the [Gallery](https://revrence-school.netlify.app/gallery.html) section of our website, where more images from the celebration have been shared.
