As we have ended the current term, we would like to extend our warmest wishes to you and your families.

Please find below the schedule regarding our upcoming break and resumption of academic activities.

School officially closed for the holidays on Thursday April 2nd. We hope this break provides a wonderful opportunity for your family to celebrate the Easter Season, reflecting on a time of renewal and joy.

We look forward to welcoming all students back to campus on **Monday April 20th, 2026**.

We encourage our students to take this time to rest and recharge for the next term. We would like to thank you for your continued partnership and support in your/our child's education.

On behalf of the entire management of Reverence Preparatory School, we wish you a safe, peaceful and happy Easter holiday.
