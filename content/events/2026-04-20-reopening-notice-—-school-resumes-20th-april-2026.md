***Dear Parents, Guardians and Students,***

We are pleased to inform you that Reverence Preparatory and JHS School will officially reopen on **Monday, 20th April 2026** for the continuation of the current academic term.

All students are expected to report to school on time, in full school uniform and with all necessary learning materials. Parents and guardians are kindly reminded to ensure their wards are adequately prepared for the resumption of academic activities.

We look forward to welcoming our students back and continuing our commitment to excellence in education.

For any enquiries, please do not hesitate to contact the school administration through our contact page or reach us directly at the school premises.

Thank you for your continued support and cooperation.

**Reverence Preparatory and JHS School Administration**Old Town, Offinso, Ashanti, Ghana
