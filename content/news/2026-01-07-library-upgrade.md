Reverence Preparatory and Junior High School has recorded another major academic milestone after emerging champions of the 2026 Northern Zone edition of the Perfect Series Basic Schools Assessment Contest.

The grand finale, held on Wednesday at Kumasi Anglican Senior High School, brought together six outstanding schools that had advanced through earlier stages of the competition. Contestants were assessed in Mathematics, English Language, Science and General Knowledge, with a strong focus on critical thinking, accuracy and speed. 

Reverence Preparatory secured first place with 378.5 points in a highly competitive contest. Unique Educational Complex placed second with 377 points, while Eldia Montessori Preparatory School finished third with 368.5 points. 

In recognition of this achievement, the school received a cash prize of GH¢6,000, a trophy, plaque, medals, certificates, citations and educational souvenirs. The victory reflects the hard work, discipline and commitment of both pupils and staff, and further strengthens Reverence Preparatory’s reputation for academic excellence. 

This achievement is a proud moment for the entire Reverence Preparatory community. It highlights the school’s dedication to quality teaching and learning, and its commitment to nurturing confident, capable and high-achieving learners.

The school congratulates all participating pupils, teachers and supporters who contributed to this remarkable success, and looks forward with confidence to the next stage of the competition.
