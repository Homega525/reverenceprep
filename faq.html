How do I apply for admission?

Complete the admissions form on the Admissions page. Our team will contact you to continue the process.

What school levels are available?

We have Pre-School, Primary, JHS, and SHS/Remedial divisions.

Are fee payment plans available?

Yes. Payment options are shared with parents during enrollment discussions.

Who can join the remedial classes?

Students preparing for SHS examinations in Science, Home Economics, or General Arts tracks.
